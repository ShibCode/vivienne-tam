Vivienne tam is entering the metaverse

Vivienne Tam, honored by Forbes magazine as one of the “25 Top Chinese-Americans in Business” and People Magazine as one of the “50 Most Beautiful People” in the world, is an international reknown designer, and taking her first steps to explore NFTs in the metaverse.

International reach

Vivienne Tam is a legendary fashion designer based in NYC and HK, who has an international reputation for bridging East and West. She has done numerous Fashion shows and collections, and some of her pieces have became permanent artworks in the Andy Warhol Museum in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, the Los Angeles's Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising Museum, the Texas Fashion Collection, and New York's Museum at the Fashion Institute of Technology.

Vivienne Tam has dressed numerous leaders in fashion, business, politics, including Dr. Jill Biden, Julia Roberts, Taylor Swift, Lady Gaga, Jessica Alba, Paris Hilton, Danielle Campbell, and Asian celebrities like Gong Li, Fan Bing Bing and Ziyi Zhang.

Her shows and designs have been watched by millions of viewers, across 170+ countries, and featured on Good Morning America, Wired, Glamour, NBC, New York Times, Elle, Fox, Variety, Esquire, Vogue, Forbes, Entertainment, Cosmopolitan, Reuters

past collaboration

Vivienne Tam is known for pushing the boundaries and crossing domains. She was the first to design a “digital clutch” computer for the runway, and through her collection and shows, have pioneered collaborations with the likes of Google, Square HP, WeChat, Motorola, Lenovo, Monster. She has also worked with numerous other global brands such as Crocs, Be@rBrick, Ferrero Rocher, Starbucks, TSL Jewelry, Georg Jensen, Mandarin Oriental, Disneyland, Barbie, Elle.

our story

Working with Project Ark, Vivienne Tam is exploring the NFT ecosystem, and looking to engage with sophisticated NFTs collectors, NFT Brands, project teams, designers, artists, and thought leaders and visionaries, to brainstorm ideas of what a new age of metaverse fashion could look like.

Designing Harmonious metaverse Bridges

Drawing from East and West creative influences, and her experience working with the world’s largest tech companies, Vivienne Tam will bring new ideas and designs to bridge the world of the physical and the digital, and uncover new ideas... .
